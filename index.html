Wavy Wave

I modified Future Splash example from paper.js website provided into wavy wave form on my photograph of the beach.
At first, I changed the color of the fill to ocean blue to be looked like the ocean.
Then, I adjust the values such as timestep and mass to give heavier wave feeling than the example that paper.js provided.
After adjustments, it does not show anymore of the shallow mood of the wavy shapes.
In addition, I added the background image which was a bit of challenge to figure it out.
Also, when the space bar key is pushed, the wave color will change for brighter blue.

I wanted to use this paper.js canvas as moody and also the user can play with wave.
Thus, I made the ocean wave flowing over my photograph of the beach with mirror of its reflection.
